about.

Indrek Linnamägi is a student from Estonian Academy of Arts studying jewellery and blacksmithing on 2 year BA.

Precision, detail and aesthetics have been in the center of his work for the last 10 years working as a dental technician.

Journey into art has given him a chance to express craftsmanship through shapes, forms and patterns.
