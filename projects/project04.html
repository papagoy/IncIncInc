title.metal casting

year.2017

material.aluminium, brass, bronze, copper

size.120x120x90 mm (small bowl) 230x230x40mm (large bowl)

supervisor.Eve-Margus Villems

about the work.this project was focused on the technical side as I chose to work with metal casting method. I was fascinated by the fact that one particular metal or alloy could be pushed to such an extreme physical parameters that it starts to melt. seeing molten metal taking solid forms instantly after being poured into a different environment is something I truly enjoy. it's like seeing the creation of the world. for the forms I used gypsum based plaster. making two sides, then wiring them together and heating them up slightly for the casting.
